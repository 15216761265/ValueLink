Our Solutions:

We understand that valuation always pose challenges. Thus, we provide tailored solutions targeting our client’s business objectives, achieving measurable results, and deploying exceptional professional skills.

Financial reporting

In Trinity, we offer a wide range of valuation advisory services for financial reporting or IPO related valuation issues. Our financial valuation team will assist you to overcome today’s financial reporting challenges. Trinity’s valuation services are structured to address your business’s financial reporting needs with the following key aspects:
Equity valuation
Employee share option valuation
Purchase price allocation and intangible assets valuation
Impairment tests of goodwill and long-term assets
Financial instruments valuation
Portfolio valuation
Property and fixed asset valuation
Incremental borrowing rate determination
Expected credit loss analysis
Hedging effectiveness analysis
We are trusted partners to major local and international auditors. Our dedication to full-range financial valuation services defines who we are as a professional services firm.

Transaction Opinions

A competent transaction opinion facilitates a successful M&A process in the aspects of time, cost and management easiness. Trinity’s transaction opinions services premise on our deep industry expertise and can help your company to clear challenges in your M&A process. The rapid decision process poses pressures on management to beat demanding schedules. Also, converting the financial impact of various decisions and terms in contracts into numerical terms can facilitate a successful outcome. We have experienced experts seeking to deliver timely, comprehensive and flexible transaction advisory solutions to our client.
We work with you to provide decision support to maximize the value from a transaction. Your company needs reliable and specific intelligence about business values and financial impacts to take informed decisions about the deals. We drill down to obtain every critical numbers through research, industry experience, and cutting-edge models, coupled with superb analysis, so that you can support your internal stakeholders and their critical decisions with confidence.

Litigation Support Services

Parties in litigation process often need professional level financial and valuation expertise to assist and support their unique needs.
